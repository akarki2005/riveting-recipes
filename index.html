Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Prep Time: 30 Minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

1/2 cup penne Pasta
2 pounds chicken breast
3 cups heavy cream
3 cups spinach
1 cup sundried tomatoes
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic
2 tablespoons butter
Italian seasoning
Salt and pepper
Olive oil

Instructions

1. In a pot, boil water over high heat, adding salt once it has come to a boil
2. Add penne pasta to pot and cook for approximately 10 minutes, then drain and set aside
3. Cut chicken breast into small chunks, then season with salt, pepper and italian seasoning
4. Fill a pan with olive oil and cook chicken over medium heat, until golden brown
5. Set chicken aside to cool, then begin the sauce by sauteing butter, garlic, spinach and tomatoes over medium heat
6. After spinach wilts, add heavy cream and let sauce simmer
7. Remove from heat and add parmesan cheese and italian seasoning, as well as salt and pepper
8. Add cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce, mixing well until everything is combined

English Muffin Sandwiches

English Muffin Sandwiches

Prep Time: 20 Minutes
Servings: 1

Ingredients

2 english muffins
2 eggs
Egg whites
Ground pork for 2 sausage patties
Sliced or shredded cheese
Salt and pepper
Brown sugar
Basil, thyme, rosemary etc.
Chili flakes
Olive oil

Instructions

1. In a small bowl, season ground pork with salt, pepper, sugar, herbs and chili flakes
2. Mix pork and seasonings well, then form 2 patties
3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and season with salt and pepper, adding extra egg whites if desired
4. In a large pan, use olive oil to cook the two patties and eggs over low-medium heat
5. Toast english muffins until golden brown
6. Assemble sandwiches, adding cheese in between egg and sausage to allow for it to melt
