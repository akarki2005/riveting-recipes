Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Prep Time: 30 Minutes
Servings: 4

Ingredients

1/2 cup penne Pasta
2 pounds chicken breast
3 cups heavy cream
3 cups spinach
1 cup sundried tomatoes
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic
2 tablespoons butter
Italian seasoning
Salt and pepper
Olive oil

Instructions

1. In a pot, boil water over high heat, adding a pinch of salt once it has come to a boil
2. Add penne pasta to pot and cook for approximately 10 minutes, then drain and set aside
3. Season chicken breast with salt, pepper and italian seasoning on both sides
4. Fill a pan with olive oil and cook chicken over medium-high heat, until golden
5. Set chicken aside to cool, then dice into bite-sized strips
6. In the same pan, begin the sauce by sauteing the butter, garlic, spinach and tomatoes over medium heat
7. After spinach wilts, add heavy cream and let sauce simmer
8. Remove from heat and add parmesan cheese and italian seasoning, as well as salt and pepper
9. Add cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce, mixing well until everything is combined
